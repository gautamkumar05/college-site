DR. SHYAMA PRASAD MUKJERJEE UNIVERSITY

It was established as a college in 1926 and upgraded to a university in 2017.

Ranchi College was established as a government intermediate college in 1926, and begun undergraduate and postgraduate courses in 1946. Following the Indian independence it operated as unit of Patna University. It became a unit of Ranchi University upon its creation in 1960 and became autonomous in 2009.[3] In 2017 it was upgraded to a state university through the Jharkhand State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2017.[4]
